General Features of DX Systems

New techniques used in low energy cost compressor systems have paved the way for high efficiency. With a Coefficient of Performance (C.O.P) reaching up to 4, DX systems have become an integral part of modern HVAC solutions. These systems provide reliable and energy-efficient cooling with minimal operational cost.

Applications and Advantages of DX Systems

DX systems offer easy installation and quick startup thanks to advanced expansion technology. Unlike traditional systems, each additional pump delivers cold air directly without requiring a separate hot water generator. This streamlined design minimizes complexity and setup time.

Higher capacities can be achieved by combining low-capacity outdoor units, making DX systems ideal for scalable applications. Their adaptability and efficiency make them suitable for a wide range of environments, from residential to large-scale industrial projects.
