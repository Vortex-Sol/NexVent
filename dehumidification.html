Dehumidification Systems

Swimming pool dehumidification units are specialized air conditioners designed to control humidity levels in enclosed pool areas. These systems extract moisture from the air, preventing condensation and ensuring a comfortable environment. In addition to dehumidification, they provide heating to maintain optimal temperature levels in the pool hall.

What are the general features of swimming pool dehumidification units?

These units are constructed with a modular frame system using galvanized steel profiles. Panels consist of double-layered steel sheets with thermal and acoustic insulation materials in between. Moisture removal circuits include condensers, evaporators, compressors, and expansion valves. Moist air passes over cold evaporator surfaces causing condensation, which is collected and drained. Fans powered by inverters circulate air efficiently. High-efficiency scroll compressors and integrated automation systems optimize performance and energy usage.

What is the purpose of swimming pool dehumidification units?

These units control the relative humidity inside enclosed pool areas, improving air quality by removing excess moisture that can cause discomfort and damage to the building structure. High humidity and temperature levels in pool environments can reduce oxygen levels and increase harmful gases, making air dehumidification critical for health and safety. Additionally, these systems help maintain optimal conditions for preserving building materials, stored goods, and even valuable documents by preventing corrosion and decay.

Where are swimming pool dehumidification units used?

These units are essential for closed swimming pools to maintain humidity within set limits while providing necessary heating. They are equipped with heating coils connected to hot water systems to regulate air temperature. The system removes moist air when indoor conditions differ from outdoor temperatures, replacing it with drier air and minimizing energy consumption by switching off compressors when appropriate. Adjustable flaps regulate fresh and exhaust airflows according to internal and external temperature differences.

What are the advantages of swimming pool dehumidification units compared to other systems?

Unlike older central air conditioning systems that exhaust indoor air and bring in cold fresh air—leading to high energy costs in colder months—pool dehumidification units recycle heat through moisture collection circuits. This method retains heat efficiently and reduces energy consumption, especially during transition and winter periods. The integrated automation system allows optimal control for energy savings and comfort.
