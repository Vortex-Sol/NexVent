Our Company

Nexvent is a leading distributor of products for the ventilation and air conditioning (HVAC) industry in Poland. The company was established at the end of 1996. Thanks to the rapid development, it has built a stable and established position on the HVAC market and has gained the reputation and trust of customers. Nexvent's mission is to support all professionals operating in the HVAC industry and to provide them with the best quality, competitively priced and practical solutions in the field of ventilation and air conditioning.
