General Features of Water-Cooled Refrigeration Groups (Chiller)

High-efficiency water-cooled (COP) refrigeration groups are manufactured in a mono-block design to meet any design needs. Thanks to their compact design, they take up less space, which means it is not necessary to have large machinery rooms. Refrigeration groups with water cooling have a wide selection of models. They are used not only for comfort purposes, but also in the industrial sector. Thanks to the multi-chiller function, they ensure uninterrupted operation. Thanks to the remote connection and network-based communication system, faults can be diagnosed quickly and easily, ensuring reliable operation. Water-cooled refrigeration groups are used in large projects, power plants and the industrial sector, as well as in many other fields.

Applications of Water-Cooled Cooling Groups (Chiller)

Refrigeration groups with water cooling have a wide range of applications. In addition to being used for comfort purposes, they are also preferred in power plants, production plants, the food sector, the mold industry and many other areas, including for sports purposes. Chilled water is easily transported to the desired location using the system's internal installations. Ready-to-use chilled water is used in mold cooling processes in manufacturing plants that require cold water, using central air conditioners and fan coil units. Thanks to the multi-chiller function and the possibility of use, they ensure reliable operation. In climatic conditions with cold winters, the free cooling function enables effective cooling with low energy consumption. Refrigeration groups with air-water cooling are manufactured as mono-block units or with separate condensers. Refrigeration groups with air and water cooling provide water at temperatures from +25 °C to -15 °C, meeting various needs.

Advantages of Refrigeration Groups (Chiller) Compared to Other Systems

Refrigeration groups are preferred in large projects. Large areas such as large airports, conference and sports halls require more power and cooling capacity, so large refrigeration units are needed. In addition, chilled water stored in insulated batteries ensures continuous cooling flow. In smaller systems where multiple units and control systems are installed, the risk of failure is greater and there is also the possibility of lack of synchronization. Thanks to one large refrigeration unit, it is possible to simultaneously cool the production line in the factory and cool the administration building. The continuous circulation of chilled water in the system allows you to quickly achieve high efficiency immediately after starting the air conditioning center and fan coil units.
